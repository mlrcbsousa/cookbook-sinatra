Strawberry Meringues

Dessert - Easy - Cheap - Vegetarian - Without Pork - Kosher
(31 votes)
Report this

Add to my
recipe book

Preparation time : 20 minutes
Cooking time : 18 minutes

Ingredients (for 6 persons) :
- 4 egg whites
- 250 g sugar
- vanilla extract
- 30 cl heavy cream
- 300 g strawberries
- slivered almonds

- 4 egg whites
- 9 oz sugar
- vanilla extract
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 11 oz strawberries
- slivered almonds

Method :


Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°C).

Beat the egg whites and the sugar gradually to get a meringue mixture.

Cover a baking tray with baking paper and oil it lightly. Pour the meringue over the baking paper and spread it out on the tray. Sprinkle slivered almonds on top.

Bake for 8 minutes, or until the top of the meringue is brown.

Lower the heat to 160°C (320°F) and bake the meringue for another 10 minutes, or until the top of the meringue is solid.

Put a sheet of baking paper onto the work surface. Take the meringue out of the oven, turn it over and let it cool down.

Whip up the cream with vanilla extract.

Pour the whipped cream on the meringue, then add some chopped strawberries. Roll the meringue with the help of the baking paper.
Place it in the fridge for at least 1 hour.

Advice :

Délicieux avec des framboises.
